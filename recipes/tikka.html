Kylie's Tikka Masala

Description

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Tikka masala sauce
  • Basmati rice
  • cumin powder
  • onion powder
  • habanero pepper powder

Steps:

  1. Take 1 cup of tikka masala sauce and pour it into skillet
  2. Add 1/2 tsp. of cumin
  3. Add 1/4 tsp. of onion powder
  4. Add 1/4 tsp. of cumin powder
  5. Depending on desired level spice, add habanero powder
  6. Stir and let cook for 30-45 minutes on medium to high on oventop
