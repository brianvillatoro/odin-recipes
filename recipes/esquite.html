Esquite Callejero

Description

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans of corn
  • 1 cup of lime mayonaise
  • 1/2 cup of ground parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp of Tajin

Steps:

  1. Take 2 cans of corn and pour them in a mixing bowl
  2. Plop that heaping cup of mayonaise into the bowl
  3. Sprinkle that parmesan cheese all over the bowl, or just in one spot
  4. Sparkle the tajin into the bowl
  5. Mix the corn concoction curating a creamy cruuuunch
